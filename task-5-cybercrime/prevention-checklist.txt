Stay Safe Online – Cybercrime Prevention Checklist

---

Account & Password Safety

* Use strong and unique passwords for every account
* Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
* Never share your passwords or OTPs with anyone
* Avoid saving passwords on public or shared devices

---

UPI & Online Payment Safety

* Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money
* Do not scan QR codes sent by unknown people
* Always verify the receiver’s UPI ID before making payments
* Avoid urgent payment requests without confirmation

---

Safe Browsing Practices

* Check website URLs carefully before logging in
* Avoid clicking on suspicious or unknown links
* Download apps only from official app stores
* Do not use public Wi-Fi for banking or transactions

---

Social Media & Communication Safety

* Do not share personal information publicly
* Be cautious of messages that create urgency or fear
* Do not trust unknown profiles or random requests
* Think before posting and maintain a positive digital footprint

---

What To Do If You Are a Victim

* Report the incident at https://cybercrime.gov.in
* Call Cybercrime Helpline: 1930 (24x7)
* Inform your bank immediately in case of fraud
* Change passwords for all affected accounts
