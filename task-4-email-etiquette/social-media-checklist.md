# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

Siddharth Sharma | 25BCE10977 | B.Tech CSE Core | VIT Bhopal University

---

## DO's

| # | Do |
|---|-----|
| 1 | **Keep your profiles professional.** |
| 2 | **Think before you post.** |
| 3 | **Use privacy settings wisely.** |
| 4 | **Give credit when sharing others' work.** |
| 5 | **Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.** |
| 6 | **Log out of shared or public devices.** |

---

## DON'Ts

| # | Don't |
|---|-------|
| 1 | **Do not share sensitive personal information (address, phone number, bank details)** |
| 2 | **Do not click on suspicious links or unknown attachments.** |
| 3 | **Do not accept friend requests from unknown or suspicious profiles.** |
| 4 | **Do not use social media excessively during academic hours.** |
| 5 | **Do not spread misinformation.** |
| 6 | **Do not over share your location or daily routine.** |
